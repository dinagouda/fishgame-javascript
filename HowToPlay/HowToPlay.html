Pong Shark is a very simple and interesting game
which You can play with simple options.
These options are listed here , click on one of them to see what you can do with it
you can win the game if you collect 15 hits :)

To move the shark up to hit the ball, you can do that by pressing upward button in keyboard. To move the shark down to hit the ball, you can do that by pressing downward button in keyboard. To start playing Shark-Pong you must press "Play" button on the window. To pause playing, you can do that by pressing "Pause" button on the window.
And to resume playing again, you can do that by pressing "Play" button on the window. At any time you want to reset the game, press "Reset" button on the window. You can reduce the movement of the ball by pressing ▼ button on the window. You can speed up the movement of the ball by pressing ▲ button on the window.
